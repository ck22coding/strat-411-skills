# Phase 4.5: TA Feedback Checkpoint — Detailed Steps

> Loaded by SKILL.md at the Phase 4.5 gate. Follow all steps in order.

---

## Step 1: Present to TA

Tell the user: "Share your mock slides file (`{case-prefix}-mock-slides.md`) and leadline decisions (`{case-prefix}-leadline-decisions.md`) with your TA. Take detailed notes on their feedback."

## Step 2: Record Feedback

Ask: "What feedback did your TA give?" Record the raw notes.

## Step 3: Gap-Analyze

Walk through each piece of TA feedback and identify what needs to change:
- Which leadlines need rewording?
- Which mock slides need restructuring?
- Any missing analyses or data points?
- Any incorrect framing?

## Step 4: Apply Changes

Update the mock-slides file. Add a changelog table at the top:

| TA Feedback | Before | After |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| [feedback point] | [original] | [revised] |

Update leadline-decisions file if any leadlines changed.

Save updated files to the working directory.

## Step 5: Confirm

Present the changes to the user. Ask: "Do these changes address your TA's feedback?"

**Do NOT proceed to Phase 5 until TA feedback is fully incorporated.**
